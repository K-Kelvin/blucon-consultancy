About Us.

Blucon Consulting Ltd

Blucon Consulting Ltd is a Pan-African advisory and management consulting firm that provides solutions in the fields of Project Management, Strategy, Research, Marketing and business sustainability solutions. We don’t believe in a one-size-fits-all approach to Consultancy. Our aim is to transform businesses from the inside, through offering cutting edge client specific solutions. We draw insight from client data, building flexible, future-oriented and resilient businesses.

Mission
Our mission is to understand the clients’ unique needs and carve out innovative and comprehensive solutions through professional consulting services
Vision
Our vision is to be recognized as an impactful, innovative and efficient Management Consulting partner
